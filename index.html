Not a planner. Not a habit app. A scaffolding system.

Divergify helps you do the part most apps ignore: starting. You check in, pick one anchor task, then get a scaffold that breaks the task into steps your nervous system can actually follow.

  • Daily check in: energy, mood, capacity
  • Anchor task: one priority that counts today
  • Scaffolds: tiny steps that reduce overwhelm and initiation paralysis
  • Beat your baseline: progress against you, not productivity influencers
