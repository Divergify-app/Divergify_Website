For brains that see patterns from a different angle.

Divergify is scaffolding for people who think differently.

We are building a practical support system for neurodivergent and borderline-neurodivergent life logistics: what to do first, what to do next, and how to keep going without burning out.

This homepage is the short version. The details live in The Hub, Guide, Field Notes, and the legal pages.

Why we are doing this

The world expects one default operating system for every brain. We know that is false. Divergify exists to build tools around real cognitive variance, not around shame.

What we are building

We are building an execution environment, not a diagnosis system. You can use Divergify to check in, map friction, pick an anchor, and run tiny steps that fit your current state.

  • Mode matching for state-aware support, including lower-stimulation options.
  • Node tracking so patterns are logged across time, not judged in one moment.
  • Temporal history support so your timeline has context instead of guilt loops.
  • A working language layer (including TA/TAK shorthand and node labels) that is still being refined with community feedback.

Legal and safety boundaries

Divergify is not medical care, mental-health treatment, crisis care, legal advice, or diagnosis. It is a productivity and organization support platform in active development.

If you need clinical support, diagnosis, or emergency care, use licensed medical services and local emergency resources.
